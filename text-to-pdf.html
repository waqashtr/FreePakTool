How to Use the Text to PDF Generator

  1. Enter your text in the text area above. You can paste text from any source.
  2. Customize the PDF settings - choose page size, orientation, font size, and margins.
  3. Generate your PDF - click the "Generate PDF" button to create your document.
  4. Preview or download - you can preview the PDF before downloading or download it directly.

Note: The PDF will preserve your text formatting including line breaks and paragraphs. For advanced formatting, consider using a word processor before converting to PDF.
