How to Use the Image Compressor

  1. Upload your image by clicking the "Select File" button or dragging and dropping your file into the upload area.
  2. Adjust compression settings - choose the quality level and output format.
  3. Compress the image - click the "Compress Image" button to reduce the file size.
  4. Download the compressed image - save the optimized image to your device.

Note: The compression process happens in your browser. Your images are not uploaded to any server, ensuring your privacy.
