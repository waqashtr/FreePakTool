How to Use the Audio to Text Converter

  1. Upload your audio file by clicking the "Select File" button or dragging and dropping your file into the upload area.
  2. Wait for processing - our tool will analyze your audio file and convert it to text.
  3. Review the text - the converted text will appear in the results section.
  4. Copy or download - you can copy the text to your clipboard or download it as a text file.

Note: For best results, use clear audio files without background noise. The accuracy depends on audio quality and speaker clarity.
