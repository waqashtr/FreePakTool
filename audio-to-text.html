How to Use the Audio to Text Converter

  1. Upload your audio file by clicking the "Select File" button or dragging and dropping your file into the upload area.
  2. Select language - choose the language spoken in your audio file.
  3. Convert to text - click the "Convert to Text" button to start transcription.
  4. Review the text - the converted text will appear in the results section.
  5. Copy or download - you can copy the text to your clipboard or download it as a text file.

Note: For best results, use clear audio files without background noise. The accuracy depends on audio quality and speaker clarity.
